## NP-hard
A problem $X$, not necessarily in the [[class of NP|NP]] class, is NP-hard if there is an [[class of NPC|NP-complete]] problem $Y$, such that $Y$ is [[reducibility|reducible]] to $X$ in polynomial time $(X \leq_p Y)$.
